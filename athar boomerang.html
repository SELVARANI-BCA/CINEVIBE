CINEVIBE - BOOMERANG

Boomerang is a 2019 Tamil-language action thriller film directed by R. Kannan and stars Atharvaa Murali, Megha Akash, Indhuja Ravichandran, and Upen Patel. The movie explores themes of identity, revenge, and social justice, with a unique storyline revolving around face transplant surgery. Atharvaa plays Siva, a young man who undergoes facial reconstruction surgery after a traumatic accident. Once he recovers, he finds himself the target of repeated attacks from strangers, eventually learning that his new face originally belonged to a man named Shakthi (also played by Atharvaa), who had enemies due to his involvement in activism against corrupt forces. Determined to uncover the truth, Siva delves into Shakthi’s past and fights to complete Shakthi’s mission.
