CINEVIBE - AVESHAM

Avesham is a 2008 Malayalam drama film directed by Sibi Malayil, starring Jayaram, Kalyani, and Salim Kumar in key roles. The film explores the life of a man named Madhavan, played by Jayaram, who faces a series of personal and professional challenges. Madhavan is struggling to balance his family life, career, and personal aspirations while dealing with complicated relationships. The film touches on themes of sacrifice, emotional turmoil, and the search for meaning in life. Avesham was appreciated for its sensitive portrayal of human emotions and relationships, as well as the strong performances by the cast.
