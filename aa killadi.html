CINEVIBE - KILLADI

Killadi is a 2022 Telugu-language action thriller film directed by Venu Madikanti, starring Sree Vishnu in the lead role. The movie is a gripping tale of survival, mystery, and action that keeps the audience on the edge of their seats. The story revolves around the character of Vasu (played by Sree Vishnu), a simple man who leads a normal life but finds himself in an extraordinary situation when he is wrongly implicated in a series of crimes. His life takes a sudden turn when he is forced to fight for survival against powerful enemies, who are after him for reasons he doesn't understand. As Vasu uncovers the truth behind the events that have trapped him, he must navigate through a world filled with danger, deception, and conspiracy.
