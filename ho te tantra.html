CINEVIBE - TANTRA

Tantra is a 2023 Telugu-language supernatural horror thriller film directed by G. S. Karthik and starring newcomers in the lead roles. The film combines elements of horror, mystery, and the occult, offering a chilling narrative that explores dark forces and the impact of black magic. The plot centers around a young couple who move into a new home in an isolated location, hoping for a peaceful life. However, soon after settling in, they begin to experience unexplained paranormal events that disturb their lives. Strange occurrences such as unexplained noises, visions, and disturbances start to escalate, leading the couple to seek help. As the story unfolds, it is revealed that the house is connected to a dark history involving witchcraft, black magic, and a powerful entity from the past. The couple learns that the spirits haunting them are the result of a tantric ritual gone wrong, which opened a portal to the supernatural world.
