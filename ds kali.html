CINEVIBE - KALI

Kali is a 2016 Malayalam psychological thriller directed by Sameer Thahir, starring Dulquer Salmaan and Sai Pallavi in lead roles. The film follows the story of Sidharth, a man with a temper issue, who is on a road trip with his wife, Anjali. During the trip, a series of unexpected events unfold, and Sidharth's anger issues become a central element as they get caught in a tense and dangerous situation. The film explores themes of rage, control, and the impact of past experiences on an individual's behavior. Kali is noted for its gripping storyline, intense performances, and well-crafted suspense.
