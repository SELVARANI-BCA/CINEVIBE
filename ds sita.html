CINEVIBE - SITA RAMAM

Sita Ramam is actually a Telugu movie, not a Malayalam one. It was released in 2022 and directed by Hanu Raghavapudi. The film is a romantic drama set in the 1960s and revolves around the love story between Lieutenant Ram, a soldier, and Sita, a woman who is distant and mysterious. Their love story is set against the backdrop of a military operation in Kashmir, with themes of war, love, and destiny. The movie was highly praised for its cinematography, music, and performances, especially that of Dulquer Salmaan, who played Ram.
