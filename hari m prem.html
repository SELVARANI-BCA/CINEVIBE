CINEVIBE - PREM POOJARI

Prem Poojari is a 1986 Indian Malayalam-language romantic drama film directed by Rajasenan and written by P. K. Balakrishnan. The film stars Mammootty in the lead role, along with Shobhana, Sreenivasan, and Jagathy Sreekumar in supporting roles. The film tells the story of Hari, played by Mammootty, a compassionate and kind-hearted man who is deeply in love with a woman named Sumathi, played by Shobhana. However, due to a series of misunderstandings, Sumathi ends up marrying someone else, leaving Hari heartbroken. The narrative explores Hari's emotional journey and his eventual growth, as he learns to move on from his unrequited love and seek happiness in different aspects of life.
