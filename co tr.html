Desingu Raja (2013) is a Tamil-language comedy film directed by Ezhil, featuring Vimal and Bindu Madhavi in the lead roles. The story revolves around two villages in constant conflict, and the plot follows the antics that ensue when the hero, Desingu Raja, tries to bridge the gap between the feuding communities. The film combines comedy, romance, and family drama, though it has received mixed reviews for its predictable storyline and forced humor​ Despite its flaws, the film's music, composed by D. Imman, gained popularity, with songs like "Ammadi Ammadi" topping charts​ WIKIPEDIA . A sequel, Desingu Raja 2, was announced in 2024, hinting at a continuation of the character's journey
