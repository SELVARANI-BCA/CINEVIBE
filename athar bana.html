CINEVIBE - BAANA KAATHADI

Baana Kaathadi is a 2010 Tamil-language romantic drama directed by Badri Venkatesh. The film stars Atharvaa Murali in his debut role as Ramesh (Ramesh “Ramesh Pithamagan”) and Samantha Ruth Prabhu as Priya. The storyline is set against the backdrop of North Chennai and revolves around the life of a carefree young man with a passion for kite flying, his love interest, and the challenges he faces due to his involvement in a series of misunderstandings and conflicts. The film’s narrative explores themes of friendship, love, and the realities of life for young people in urban neighborhoods. Ramesh falls in love with Priya, but their relationship faces unexpected turns due to misunderstandings and societal issues. The movie balances lighthearted romance with moments of drama, creating an emotional arc for the characters.
