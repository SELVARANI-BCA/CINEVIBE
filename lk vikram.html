CINEVIBE - VIKRAM

Vikram (2022) is a high-octane action thriller directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj, featuring Kamal Haasan in the titular role. This film is set in a world of undercover operations, where Vikram, a former black-ops commander, is drawn into a deadly battle against a drug syndicate led by a ruthless kingpin named Santhanam. As the plot unfolds, Vikram teams up with a group of vigilantes to take down this powerful network, facing several intense challenges along the way. The film is notable for its gripping narrative, intense action sequences, and stellar performances, particularly from Kamal Haasan, Vijay Sethupathi, and Fahadh Faasil. The movie was a massive commercial success and gained a dedicated following due to its fast-paced storytelling and complex characters.
