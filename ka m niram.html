CINEVIBE - NIRAM

Niram is a Malayalam romantic drama film released in 1999, directed by Kamal. The film stars Kunchacko Boban and Shalini in the lead roles and was a significant hit at the time of its release. The story revolves around the blossoming love between two characters, Giri and Anjali, who are childhood friends but grow up with a complex relationship. The movie focuses on themes of love, friendship, and the challenges that come with growing up and realizing feelings. Giri is a carefree and charming young man who harbors a secret love for his childhood friend, Anjali, who is initially unaware of his feelings. Their journey is filled with misunderstandings, emotional hurdles, and the realization of their true feelings for each other. The film beautifully explores the innocence of first love and the emotional turmoil that accompanies it. Niram is known for its memorable songs, particularly the romantic tracks that became iconic.
