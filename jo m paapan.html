CINEVIBE - PAAPAN

Paapan is a 2002 Indian Malayalam-language drama film directed by Rajasenan and written by C.G. Rajendra Babu. The film stars Jayaram, Sreekala, Innocent, and Kuthiravattam Pappu in significant roles. The film follows the life of Paapan (played by Jayaram), a man who lives a simple life but finds himself caught up in a series of personal and emotional crises. Paapan is an innocent, good-hearted individual, but he faces numerous challenges as he tries to navigate complex relationships with his family and society. The movie deals with themes of human relationships, guilt, and redemption. While the storyline is grounded in family drama and humor, the film also explores the inner turmoil and moral dilemmas faced by the protagonist.
