CINEVIBE - JAWAN

Jawan (2023), directed by Atlee, is an intense action thriller that blends mystery, drama, and crime. The film stars Shah Rukh Khan in a dual role as Vikram Rathore, a commando, and his son Azad Rathore, who seeks justice for the oppressed. The story spans across a gripping plot where Azad confronts powerful antagonist Kalee Gaikwad, played by Vijay Sethupathi, and fights against political corruption, environmental damage, and societal injustices. The film explores themes of revenge, justice, and familial bonds, set against a backdrop of high-octane action sequences and emotional drama. The film also features a strong supporting cast, including Nayanthara as Narmada, Azad's wife, and Deepika Padukone in a special role. With its intense narrative, Jawan has received praise for its action choreography, performances, and social messaging.
