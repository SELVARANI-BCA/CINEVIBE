CINEVIBE - THANIORUVAN

Thani Oruvan is a Tamil action thriller about Mithran, an honest IPS officer, who faces off against an intelligent and ruthless scientist, Siddharth Abhimanyu. The film explores the battle of wits between the two, with Mithran determined to expose Siddharth's corrupt practices. The film is notable for its gripping narrative, intense action sequences, and the strong performances by the cast, including Jayam Ravi as Mithran and Arvind Swamy as Siddharth. It delves into themes of justice, morality, and deception.
