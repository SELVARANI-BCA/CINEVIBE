CINEVIBE - RAJA THE GREAT

Raja the Great (2017) is a Telugu-language action-comedy film directed by Anil Ravipudi and starring Ravi Teja in the lead role. The film also features Raashi Khanna as the female lead, with supporting roles played by Rajendra Prasad, Vennela Kishore, and Sampath Raj. The story follows Raja (played by Ravi Teja), a blind man who has exceptional fighting skills and an incredible sense of hearing. Despite his blindness, Raja dreams of becoming a police officer and leading a fearless life. He is an optimistic and brave person, and his unique abilities make him an unlikely hero.
