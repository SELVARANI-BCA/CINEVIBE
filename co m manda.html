CINEVIBE - MANDAKINI

"Mandakini" is a 2024 Malayalam-language drama directed by Vinod Leela. The story revolves around Aromal (Althaf Salim) and Ambili (Anarkali Marikar), a newlywed couple, whose lives take an unexpected turn on their wedding night when Ambili reveals her troubled past involving Sujith (Ganapathi S. Poduval). This revelation leads to a series of events that confront family secrets and emotional conflicts.
