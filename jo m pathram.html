CINEVIBE - PATHRAM

Pathram is a 1999 Indian Malayalam-language political thriller film directed by Raja Sabapathy and written by C.G. Rajendra Babu. The film stars Mammootty in the lead role, with Ranjitha, Sukumari, Thilakan, and Nedumudi Venu in supporting roles. The story of Pathram revolves around Raghuraman (played by Mammootty), a young and idealistic journalist who is passionate about exposing corruption and societal issues. He becomes embroiled in a political conspiracy after publishing an investigative report that exposes the corrupt practices of powerful political figures and businessmen. As he uncovers a larger conspiracy, Raghuraman finds himself facing threats to his life and career, and he must decide whether to continue his pursuit of truth or succumb to the pressures of power.
