CINEVIBE - ARANMANAI

Aranmanai (2014) is a Tamil horror-comedy film directed by Sundar C. The movie features a star-studded cast including Sundar C, Hansika Motwani, Andrea Jeremiah, and Vinay Rai. The plot revolves around a family that is haunted by a vengeful spirit in their ancestral palace. The protagonist, played by Sundar C, is tasked with uncovering the mystery behind the ghost’s presence and resolving the conflicts of the past. The film blends traditional horror elements with comedy, typical of Sundar C’s directorial style, and has been well-received for its engaging screenplay, balanced mix of scares and humor, and strong performances. The movie's success led to sequels, continuing the haunted family saga. Aranmanai is noted for its spooky atmosphere, gripping sequences, and the occasional comic relief that lightens the tension​
