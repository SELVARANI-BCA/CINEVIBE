CINEVIBE - KAAPAN

Kaapan is an action-packed Tamil film featuring Suriya as the protagonist, a head of an elite security unit tasked with protecting high-profile individuals. The film revolves around a mix of political conspiracy, action sequences, and a personal vendetta. Directed by K.V. Anand, it also stars Mohanlal, Sayyeshaa, and others, and delves into themes of duty, loyalty, and sacrifice. The plot focuses on the challenges faced by the protagonist in maintaining peace amidst powerful adversaries.
