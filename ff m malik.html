CINEVIBE - MALIK

Malik is a 2021 Malayalam political thriller film directed by Mahesh Narayanan, starring Fahadh Faasil in the lead role. The film follows the life of Sulaiman Malik, a man who rises from humble beginnings to become a powerful and influential figure in his community. Set against the backdrop of social and political turbulence, Malik spans several decades and explores Malik's journey, his involvement in local politics, and the complex relationships with his family, friends, and enemies. The film touches on themes of power, loyalty, betrayal, and justice, while also delving into the socio-political issues affecting the community. Malik is notable for its engaging storytelling, strong performances—particularly by Fahadh Faasil—and its compelling narrative structure that spans multiple time periods. The film's use of non-linear storytelling and its intricate character arcs received widespread critical acclaim, making it one of the standout Malayalam films of 2021.
