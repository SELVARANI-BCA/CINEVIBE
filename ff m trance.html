CINEVIBE - TRANCE

Trance is a 2020 Malayalam psychological drama film directed by Anwar Rasheed, starring Fahadh Faasil in the lead role. The film follows the story of a man named Viju, a failed motivational speaker who is recruited by a mysterious organization to become a 'godman' and manipulate people's beliefs for profit. As Viju's life takes a dramatic turn, he struggles with his inner turmoil and the psychological effects of his new identity. Trance explores themes of spirituality, human manipulation, and the search for truth, blending elements of psychological thriller and drama. The film is known for its intense storytelling, experimental narrative style, and Fahadh Faasil's powerful performance. It was both critically acclaimed and commercially successful, becoming a cult favorite for its bold exploration of contemporary issues.
