CINEVIBE - HEY SINAMIKA

Hey Sinamika is a 2022 Tamil romantic drama film directed by Brinda, starring Dulquer Salmaan, Aditi Rao Hydari, and Kajal Aggarwal in lead roles. The story revolves around Mouna (Aditi Rao Hydari), a weather scientist, and Yaazhan (Dulquer Salmaan), her quirky and overly talkative husband. After a few years of marriage, Mouna becomes frustrated with Yaazhan's constant chatter and decides to separate from him. To execute her plan, she enlists the help of Dr. Malarvizhi (Kajal Aggarwal), but things take unexpected turns when relationships and emotions become complicated. The film is noted for its lighthearted yet emotional narrative, colorful visuals, and impressive performances by the lead actors. Hey Sinamika explores themes of love, misunderstandings, and self-discovery in modern relationships.
