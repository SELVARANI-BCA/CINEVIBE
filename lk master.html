CINEVIBE - MASTER

Master (2021) is a Tamil action-thriller film directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj, featuring Vijay in the lead role as JD, a college professor who also has a troubled past. The film also stars Vijay Sethupathi as the antagonist, Bhavani, a ruthless gangster. The story revolves around JD, who is sent to a juvenile school to reform troubled youth, only to face off against Bhavani, a gangster who controls the juvenile facility. The film's core themes revolve around redemption, the battle between good and evil, and the power of education to change lives. JD, with his unconventional methods, tries to bring about a transformation in the students, while facing fierce opposition from Bhavani. The film is celebrated for its high-octane action sequences, strong performances, especially from Vijay and Vijay Sethupathi, and its engaging screenplay. The movie also blends social messages with mass appeal, making it both a critical and commercial success. .
