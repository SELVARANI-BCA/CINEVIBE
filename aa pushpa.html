CINEVIBE - PUSHPA

Pushpa: The Rise is a 2021 Telugu-language action thriller film directed by Sukumar, starring Allu Arjun in the lead role. The film is set in the backdrop of the red sandalwood smuggling business in the forests of Andhra Pradesh, India. Allu Arjun plays the character Pushpa Raj, a rebellious and daring man who rises from humble beginnings to become a powerful figure in the world of illegal red sandalwood trade. The film follows Pushpa’s journey as he navigates through challenges, rivalries, and betrayal to establish his dominance in the criminal world. His character is marked by his fierce attitude, sharp wit, and determination. The narrative is driven by Pushpa’s fight against corrupt officials, criminal gangs, and his personal struggles, including his complicated relationship with a woman named Srivalli. The film also portrays his rising conflict with Bhanwar Singh Shekhawat , a tough officer sent to dismantle the illegal smuggling operation.
