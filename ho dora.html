Dora (2017) is a Tamil horror-thriller film starring Nayanthara as Pavalakkodi, a woman who inherits a vintage car from her deceased father. The car is believed to be haunted, with its previous owners meeting tragic fates. Initially hesitant, Pavalakkodi decides to keep the car and soon discovers that it harbors a vengeful spirit seeking retribution for past wrongs. The film explores themes of revenge, the supernatural, and a chilling quest for truth. Directed by Doss Ramasamy, the movie blends eerie suspense with a touch of humor through Thambi Ramaiah's role as Pavalakkodi's uncle. Nayanthara's performance has been widely praised for bringing strength and vulnerability to her character, making it a compelling watch.
