CINEVIBE - DARLING

Darling is a 2010 Telugu-language romantic drama film directed by Karunakaran and starring Prabhas and Kajal Aggarwal in the lead roles. The film blends romance with light-hearted comedy and emotional moments, making it a heartwarming and entertaining watch. The story revolves around a carefree and fun-loving young man named Darling (played by Prabhas), who is an easygoing person with no major ambitions in life. He is known for his charm and wit, often getting into trouble with his friends and family due to his carefree attitude. However, everything changes when he meets the beautiful and spirited Geetha (played by Kajal Aggarwal). Geetha is a college student who initially dislikes Darling due to his casual behavior, but over time, she is drawn to his sincerity and charm. The two develop a romantic relationship, with Darling trying his best to win her heart.
