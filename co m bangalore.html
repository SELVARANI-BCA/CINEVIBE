CINEVIBE - BANGALORE DAYS

"Bangalore Days" (2014) is a Malayalam romantic drama directed by Anjali Menon. It follows the lives of three close cousins—Arjun (Dulquer Salmaan), Divya (Nazriya Nazim), and Kuttan (Nivin Pauly)—who move to Bangalore for different reasons, each chasing their dreams and dealing with personal struggles. The film captures themes of friendship, love, self-discovery, and family dynamics against the vibrant backdrop of Bangalore city. Known for its stellar performances, soulful music, and heartwarming narrative, Bangalore Days became a landmark film in Malayalam cinema.
