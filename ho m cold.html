CINEVIBE - COLD CASE

"Cold Case" is a 2021 Malayalam-language supernatural crime thriller directed by Tanu Balak, starring Prithviraj Sukumaran and Aditi Balan in lead roles. The movie blends investigative drama with elements of the supernatural. The story follows ACP Satyajith (Prithviraj), who is tasked with solving a complex murder mystery after a skull is discovered in a bag. Parallelly, a journalist named Medha (Aditi Balan) moves into a new house and experiences eerie paranormal activities. The two narratives eventually converge, revealing dark secrets about the murder and the supernatural presence haunting Medha. The film stands out for its unique attempt to combine police procedural storytelling with horror elements. While the performances of the lead actors were praised, critics had mixed opinions on the pacing and execution. The movie is available for streaming on Amazon Prime Video.
