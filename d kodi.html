CINEVIBE - KODI

Kodi is a 2016 Tamil-language political thriller directed by R. S. Durai Senthilkumar. The film features Dhanush in dual roles as Kodi and Anbu, twin brothers with contrasting personalities. Trisha Krishnan plays the lead female role as Rudhra, marking her first portrayal of a negative character, while Anupama Parameswaran plays Malathi, Anbu's love interest. Kodi is an ambitious and bold young man deeply involved in politics, while Anbu is a soft-spoken teacher who avoids political conflicts. The film explores themes of political ambition, family loyalty, and betrayal as Kodi falls in love with Rudhra, a politically active woman with her own ambitions and secrets. When their political paths cross, the story takes a dark turn, revealing how power and ambition can impact relationships and lead to unexpected consequences.
