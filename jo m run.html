CINEVIBE - RUN BABY RUN

Run Baby Run is a 2012 Indian Malayalam-language thriller film directed by Joshiy and written by Sachi-Sethu. The film stars Mammootty in the lead role, with Nayanthara playing the female lead, supported by a talented cast including Lalu Alex, Saikumar, Vijayaraghavan, and others. The story revolves around Raghuraman, played by Mammootty, a cameraman working for a news channel. The plot kicks off when Raghuraman is sent to cover a high-profile political case, leading him into a web of conspiracy, suspense, and danger. He becomes involved in the investigation of a powerful politician's corruption, and the events escalate as he uncovers dangerous truths. Throughout the film, Raghuraman faces several threats to his life, both from the political world and from the media industry, as he tries to get to the bottom of the case.
