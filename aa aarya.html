CINEVIBE - AARYA

Aarya is a 2004 Telugu-language action-romantic film directed by Sukumar, starring Allu Arjun in the lead role. The movie is known for its engaging storyline, strong performances, and memorable music, and it helped establish Allu Arjun as a prominent actor in the Telugu film industry. The film follows the character Aarya (played by Allu Arjun), a fun-loving and carefree man who is known for his strong will and determination. He comes from a rural background and moves to the city, where he befriends Sreenu (played by Rajiv Kanakala), a man who has a crush on the same woman, Geetha (played by Anuradha Mehta). Despite Sreenu's affections for Geetha, Aarya falls for her as well and soon competes with him for her love.
