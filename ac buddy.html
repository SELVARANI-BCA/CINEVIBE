CINEVIBE - BUDDY

Buddy is a Telugu-language romantic drama film released in 2022. The film revolves around the life of two childhood friends, Arjun and Bhargavi, whose bond evolves as they grow older. Arjun, played by actor Sree Vishnu, is a carefree and adventurous individual who dreams of becoming successful in life, while Bhargavi, portrayed by actress Sree Leela, is a more grounded and responsible character. As the story progresses, Arjun and Bhargavi's friendship deepens, and they begin to realize their feelings for each other. However, circumstances and misunderstandings create challenges in their relationship. The film explores themes of love, friendship, sacrifice, and personal growth as both characters navigate through various hurdles in their lives. The film features a mix of emotional drama, music, and light-hearted moments, making it a feel-good movie that appeals to fans of romance and family drama.
