CINEVIBE - MANTHRIKAN

Manthrikan is a 2012 Indian Malayalam-language fantasy horror film directed by Ranjith Sankar and written by C.G. Rajendra Babu. The film stars Mammootty in the lead role, with Sana Khan, Aju Varghese, Suraj Venjaramoodu, and P. R. O. Zubair in supporting roles. The story follows Manthrikan, a man with mystical powers, played by Mammootty, who is a "tantri" (a traditional priest with supernatural abilities) in a small village. He is called upon to solve a series of mysterious events that have been plaguing the village, including reports of ghostly occurrences and other paranormal activities. The plot revolves around the spiritual and supernatural elements, with the character of Manthrikan using his knowledge of ancient rituals and mysticism to uncover the truth behind these strange happenings.
