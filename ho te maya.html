CINEVIBE - MAYA

Maya is a 2015 Telugu-language supernatural horror film directed by Ashwin Saravanan, starring Nayantara and Aari in the lead roles. The film is known for its suspenseful narrative, eerie atmosphere, and thrilling twists, combining elements of supernatural horror with psychological drama. The story revolves around the character of Maya (played by Nayantara), a young woman who works as a photographer. She begins to experience a series of strange and unsettling events that seem to be connected to her work. Maya is drawn into a mysterious situation after a chance encounter with a haunted mansion and a series of eerie photographs that she takes, which capture disturbing images linked to supernatural forces.
