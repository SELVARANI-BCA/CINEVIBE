CINEVIBE - FOUR FRIENDS

Four Friends is a Malayalam film released in 2010, directed by Saji Surendran. The movie is a drama-comedy that revolves around the lives of four childhood friends—Madhav, Krishnan, Raghuraman, and Vinod—who are reunited after many years. They have gone their separate ways in life but come back together due to a common need to attend a wedding. The film highlights their journey of rekindling old friendships and revisiting the memories of their past while dealing with the issues and responsibilities they have accumulated over the years. As they spend time together, they confront their emotional baggage, unresolved conflicts, and learn about each other's growth and changes in life. The movie touches on themes of friendship, love, regrets, and the importance of maintaining relationships despite life's challenges. It blends humor with emotional depth, making it an entertaining yet heartwarming film.
