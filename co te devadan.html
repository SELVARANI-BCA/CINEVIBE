CINEVIBE - DEVADAN

Devadan is a 2023 Telugu-language thriller film directed by S. V. Krishna Reddy. The movie stars Sree Vishnu and Priyanka Jawalkar in the lead roles and offers a blend of suspense, mystery, and drama. The film revolves around a man named Arjun (played by Sree Vishnu), who leads a peaceful life in a small town. His life takes a drastic turn when he encounters a series of unexplained events that seem to revolve around a woman named Devadan (played by Priyanka Jawalkar). Devadan is a mysterious character with a hidden past, and her involvement in Arjun's life creates a whirlwind of emotional turmoil, confusion, and danger.
