CINEVIBE - LOKPAL

Lokpal is a 2013 Indian Malayalam-language action thriller film directed by Joshiy and written by S. N. Swamy. The film stars Mammootty in the lead role, with a supporting cast that includes Mukesh, Anoop Menon, Mithun Ramesh, and others. The film follows Lokpal, an honest and fearless police officer, played by Mammootty, who is known for his ability to take on powerful criminals and corrupt individuals. The movie’s premise is built around the theme of anti-corruption and justice, with the protagonist battling against political and corporate corruption that threatens the welfare of the common man. Lokpal’s character is a symbol of integrity, standing up against those in power who exploit the system for personal gain.
