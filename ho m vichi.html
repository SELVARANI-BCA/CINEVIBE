CINEVIBE - VICHITHRAM

Vichithram is a 2022 Malayalam mystery thriller directed by Achu Vijayan. The film features a unique blend of family drama and horror, focusing on Jasmine and her five sons, who relocate to their ancestral mansion after the unexpected death of a relative. The house becomes the center of eerie and mysterious events that intertwine supernatural elements with emotional family dynamics. The cast includes Shine Tom Chacko, Kani Kusruti, Balu Varghese, and Jolly Chirayath, who deliver nuanced performances. The movie explores themes of home, relationships, and freedom, incorporating symbolic imagery such as trapped animals and insects to reflect its core narrative. Critics appreciated its fresh take on horror and storytelling, balancing emotional depth with chilling sequences.
