CINEVIBE - PARINAYAM

Parinayam is a 1994 Indian Malayalam-language film directed by P. G. Viswambharan and written by C.G. Rajendra Babu. The film stars Suresh Gopi, Shobhana, and Sreenivasan in pivotal roles. The movie revolves around the traditional customs of arranged marriage and the conflict between individual desires and familial obligations. The story follows Suresh Gopi's character, a man who is involved in a love marriage, facing societal pressures and personal challenges. Shobhana plays the role of a strong-willed woman who strives to balance her aspirations with the societal expectations around her. The title "Parinayam," which means "marriage," explores themes of love, relationships, and the impact of tradition on modern marriages. The film delves into the emotional struggles of characters who must navigate between love and the demands of their families, and it highlights how personal choices are influenced by social conventions.
