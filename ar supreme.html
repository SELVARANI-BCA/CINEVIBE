CINEVIBE - SUPREME

Supreme (2016) is a Telugu-language action-comedy film directed by Anil Ravipudi. The film stars Sai Dharam Tej in the lead role, with Rashi Khanna playing the female lead. It also features Rajendra Prasad, Posani Krishna Murali, Vennela Kishore, and others in supporting roles. The story revolves around Sai Dharam Tej as Supreme (Sreenivasa Rao), a fun-loving and good-hearted young man. He is known for his sense of justice and willingness to stand up for the weak. His life takes a turn when he is mistaken for a man who is involved in a serious crime and becomes the target of powerful people, including a notorious gangster. Sreenivasa, however, is not an ordinary man and uses his wit, charm, and courage to take on the criminals. His journey involves a series of comical and action-packed sequences as he fights for justice and clears his name. Along the way, he falls in love with Raashi Khanna's character, who adds a romantic angle to the story.
