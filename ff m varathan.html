CINEVIBE - VARATHAN

Varathan is a 2018 Malayalam action thriller film directed by Amal Neerad, starring Fahadh Faasil and Surabhi Lakshmi in lead roles. The film tells the story of Aby, a man who moves with his wife to a remote estate in Kerala after facing professional and personal setbacks. However, their peaceful life is disturbed when Aby's wife is threatened by local goons, and Aby is forced to confront his past and the dark secrets of the place. Varathan explores themes of survival, revenge, and justice, with intense action sequences and a gripping narrative. Fahadh Faasil's performance was particularly praised for its intensity, and the film received positive reviews for its atmospheric tension and strong direction.
