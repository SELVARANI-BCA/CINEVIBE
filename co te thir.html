CINEVIBE - 35

35 is a 2023 Telugu-language film directed by Venkat Reddy and starring Sree Vishnu and Priyanka Jawalkar in the lead roles. The movie is a romantic drama that explores the complexities of relationships, personal growth, and the emotional struggles that come with love and life. The plot revolves around the character of Surya (played by Sree Vishnu), a man who is 35 years old and finds himself at a crossroads in his life. Surya is dealing with the challenges of being at an age where he feels the weight of his unfulfilled dreams and relationships. He is in love with a woman named Anjali (played by Priyanka Jawalkar), but the dynamics of their relationship are strained due to various personal and professional issues. As the film progresses, it delves into Surya's internal struggles, his desire for change, and his attempts to reconcile with his past.
