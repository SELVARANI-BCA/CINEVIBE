CINEVIBE - MANAGARAM

Maanagaram (2017) is a Tamil-language action thriller film directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj in his directorial debut. The film weaves multiple storylines together in a fast-paced, interconnected narrative, capturing a series of events that unfold in Chennai over the course of a single night. The story revolves around four characters whose lives intertwine unexpectedly. Sundeep Kishan plays Muthu, an IT employee who arrives in Chennai, Sri, an aspiring actor played by Shri, and a group of individuals whose personal stories get caught up in a violent, criminal conspiracy. As their paths cross, the film explores the ripple effect of one bad decision, creating a tense atmosphere filled with suspense.
