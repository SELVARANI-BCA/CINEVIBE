CINEVIBE - OK KANMANI

OK Kanmani (short for O Kadhal Kanmani) is a 2015 Tamil romantic drama film directed by Mani Ratnam, starring Dulquer Salmaan and Nithya Menen in lead roles. The film tells the story of Adi and Tara, two young, ambitious individuals living in Mumbai who fall in love but are skeptical about marriage due to their career aspirations and modern outlook on relationships. They decide to live together, navigating the challenges of balancing love and independence. The movie beautifully captures urban romance and explores themes of commitment, career priorities, and emotional growth. OK Kanmani is celebrated for its refreshing portrayal of contemporary relationships, the chemistry between the lead actors, A.R. Rahman's soulful music, and Mani Ratnam's masterful direction. It was widely appreciated by audiences and critics alike and became a major success.
