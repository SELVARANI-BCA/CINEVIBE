CINEVIBE - ISHA

The Malayalam movie Isha, directed by Jose Thomas, is a horror thriller that revolves around themes of possession and paranormal activity. The story begins with the mysterious death of a young girl, Isha, in a hill station. The narrative then shifts to Angel, a little girl who begins to exhibit strange behavior due to possession by Isha's spirit, seeking revenge for her death. The film delves into the struggles of Angel's family as they attempt to rid her of the spirit and unravel the mystery surrounding Isha's demise. The movie features a cast that includes Baby Avani, Margaret Antony, Abhishek Vinod, and Kishor Sathya. Despite aiming to redefine the horror genre with new elements, critics noted that the screenplay follows a familiar path with predictable outcomes. The background score by Aswin Johnson effectively enhances the eerie atmosphere of the film.
