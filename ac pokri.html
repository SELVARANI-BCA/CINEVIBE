CINEVIBE - POKRI

Pokiri is a popular Telugu-language action film released in 2006, directed by Puri Jagannadh and starring Mahesh Babu in the lead role. The movie is a remake of the Tamil film Pokkiri (2007). The film follows the story of a ruthless and enigmatic gangster, Pandu (played by Mahesh Babu), who is involved in various criminal activities, including extortion and murder. However, Pandu has a secret life, and his true motives are gradually revealed as the plot unfolds. Despite his violent lifestyle, Pandu shows a softer side, especially when he falls in love with Shruti (played by Ileana D'Cruz), a woman who is unaware of his criminal background. The story Pandu getting caught in a web of crime, deceit, and betrayal. The movie is known for its high-octane action sequences, catchy music, and Mahesh Babu's charismatic performance. His portrayal of a cool and fearless character became iconic and contributed significantly to his stardom in Telugu cinema.
