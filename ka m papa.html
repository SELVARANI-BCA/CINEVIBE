CINEVIBE - PAPANASAM

Papanasam is a 2015 Malayalam film directed by Jeethu Joseph, and it is the official remake of the Tamil film Drishyam. The movie stars Mohanlal as the protagonist, Georgekutty, alongside Meena in the role of his wife, Rani. The film follows the story of Georgekutty, a cable TV operator, who lives with his wife and two daughters in a peaceful village. The family is put to the test when a tragic incident involving a young woman occurs, leading to a situation that threatens to destroy their lives. Georgekutty, known for his sharp wit and resourcefulness, must come up with an elaborate plan to protect his family from the consequences of the incident. The movie focuses on his efforts to cover up the crime, while dealing with law enforcement and the emotional turmoil it causes.
