CINEVIBE - GURUVAYOOR AMBALANADAYIL

"Guruvayoor Ambalanadayil" (2024) is a Malayalam comedy-drama film directed by Vipin Das and written by Deepu Pradeep. The movie revolves around Anandan (Prithviraj Sukumaran) and Vinu (Basil Joseph), who are on the verge of becoming brothers-in-law. However, chaos ensues when Anandan decides to disrupt the wedding plans, leading to a series of comedic errors. The story is set against the backdrop of a grand wedding at the Guruvayoor Temple, incorporating family dynamics, cultural elements, and humorous twists. Nikhila Vimal and Anaswara Rajan play significant roles in the ensemble cast. The film is celebrated for its light-hearted storytelling, strong performances, and engaging music composed by Ankit Menon.
