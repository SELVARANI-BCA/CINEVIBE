CINEVIBE - DEMONTE COLONY

Demonte Colony (2015) is a Tamil horror-thriller directed by R. Ajay Gnanamuthu in his directorial debut. The movie features Arulnithi as the lead, supported by Ramesh Thilak, Sananth, and Abhishek Joseph. Set in Chennai's eerie Demonte Colony, it revolves around four friends who enter a supposedly haunted bungalow for fun but soon find themselves trapped in a terrifying and inescapable nightmare. The film is known for its serious tone, avoiding horror-comedy trends, and building tension with a gripping narrative. Its minimalistic approach—largely set in a single location—alongside an atmospheric score by Keba Jeremiah, amplifies the sense of dread. It stands out for its lack of clichéd horror elements and a tight screenplay that keeps viewers on edge till the end​
