CINEVIBE - IRUL

"Irul" (2021) is a Malayalam mystery thriller directed by Naseef Yusuf Izuddin, featuring Fahadh Faasil, Soubin Shahir, and Darshana Rajendran in the lead roles. The film is a minimalist suspense drama that unravels over a single rainy night, with the majority of the story set inside an isolated house. The plot begins with Alex (Soubin Shahir), an author who plans a surprise getaway with his girlfriend Archana (Darshana Rajendran). However, their evening takes a dark turn when their car breaks down, and they seek refuge in a nearby house. There, they encounter a mysterious stranger (Fahadh Faasil), and the night quickly spirals into a psychological game of secrets, deception, and survival.
